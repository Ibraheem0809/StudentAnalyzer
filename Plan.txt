**Student Performance Analysis Dashboard**

**Libraries Used**

✅ Python
✅ OOP
✅ NumPy
✅ Pandas
✅ Matplotlib
✅ Seaborn
✅ Statistics concepts

No machine learning required.

**Objectives**

Analyze student data and answer questions such as:

What is the average score?
Which subject has the highest average marks?
Which students are top performers?
How are study hours related to marks?
Are there any outliers?
What factors seem to influence performance?
